Getting Around Asuncion

Ride Hailing

The best app for Asuncion is Bolt. More cars, and prices are lower than Uber. A ride to the airport 40,000-50,000 ₲, and the terminal is about 25,000₲

Busses

Busses in town go everywhere They don't take cash or debit. Buy a Jaha bus card and charge it at any Aqui Pago or Pago Express. Try the farmacy onthe corner (to the left when you leave the hostel). A ride in town costs 3400 PYG.
